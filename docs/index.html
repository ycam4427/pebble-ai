The important part

The AI never touches your files directly

The model can only output proposed actions. A separate safety validator — the only code that can authorize anything — checks every one against protected folders, your sandbox, and risk tiers. It's even enforced by the type system, so an unapproved action cannot reach your disk.

You → Pebble → Safety Validator ⛨ → Your approval → Done

FAQ

Is it really free and private?

Yes. Pebble is free and open source, and runs entirely on your computer through your local Ollama. Your files and chats never leave your PC — there's no cloud and no account.

Windows says "Windows protected your PC" / my antivirus flagged it — is it a virus?

No — it's a false positive. Pebble is an open, unsigned app (code-signing certificates cost money). To run it, click More info → Run anyway on the SmartScreen prompt, or add an exclusion in Windows Security. You can also build it yourself from source. It only talks to your local Ollama; nothing else leaves your machine.

Can it delete my files by mistake?

It's built to make that very hard. Pebble can only propose actions; an independent validator blocks system folders and needs your approval for any change. Deletes go to a recoverable Trash, and every action can be undone.

My PC is powerful — can Pebble be smarter?

Absolutely. On launch Pebble checks your hardware and recommends a stronger model. In Settings you can pull and switch to bigger models (like llama3.1:8b or qwen2.5:14b) for much better organizing and instruction-following.

Which file do I download?

The setup .exe installer — it bundles everything (including WebView2) and makes shortcuts. There's also a portable .exe if you'd rather not install.
